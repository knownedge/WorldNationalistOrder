The World Nationalist Order is founded on the principle of voluntary collaboration, generosity and gratitude. This means that it is starting from absolutely nothing and it's growth and success will depend entirely on contributions from the people who find the mission worthwile and support the project.

The first milestone to reach and the most important one is the aquisition of a suitable plot of land so that we may begin building our first micronation. Ideally we'd like to acquire a large undevelopped plot of land and build it all from scratch or maybe buy a ghost town and start from there or buy an island somewhere. It all really depends on how much support we get. Once land is acquired and we begin building, we will record our progress and stream it online for all to see. We will share our experience and teach what we learn and know. Once the place is livable and sustainable, we will begin inviting people to join us who are interested in living the pioneer lifestyle to come live and build with us.

Contributions are not limited to financial donations, you may donate property you do not use or no longer need. Tools, equipment, vehicules, food, clothing, land, etc... Such donated property will then be owned by the king and made available to the subjects of the kingdom where needed. You can contact the king at
freemandan @ protonmail.com or on telegram at https://t.me/freemandan if you wish to donate property.
